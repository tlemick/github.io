Group journeys @ WeTravel

Component Redesign

Client: WeTravel

  • UX Design
  • Research
  • Interaction Design

the Project

WeTravel helps travelers find other world adventurers. Our brief was to investigate why potential travelers would drop out of the flow once they got to the purchase screen and develop solutions for a higher conversion rate.

the Work

We first spent some time breaking down the existing flow and creating hypotheses on why users might drop off.

The three areas we theorized needed work were breaking down the complexity of group travel, confidence that the group of travelers is the right fit for the user, and general excitement generation.

Testing

We developed a testing program and brought in 10 people per round. After each round we'd analyze the data, make improvements and test again. After 4 rounds we had over 90% confidence in for identified areas.

Launch

WeTravel approved the work and the flow was launched. Checking in a year later confirmed that the findings were valid and bookings were up.
