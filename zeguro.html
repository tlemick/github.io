Cyber defense @ Zeguro

A cyber-security risk platform

Role: Head of Design

  • UX Design
  • Product Ideation
  • Copywriting
  • Interaction Design
  • Branding
  • Marketing

the Project

Zeguro seeks to help fortify small and medium businesses against cyber-security threats. We developed a platform to reduce risk through education, testing, scanning infrastructure, and creating and establishing security policies.

Companies that use the platform to reduce their risk are rewarded with reduced rates from the insurance wing of the company.

the Work

I was Zeguro's first designer and collaborated with the founders on features sets, creating mockups based on those features, and testing them with potential users.

After the MVP began development in earnest I continued testing and refining, while starting to build visual and brand identities for the platform.

As complexity increased, I also started development of our design system and component library to streamline the work of our engineers and maintain consistency across products.
