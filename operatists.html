the Project

One of the most powerful cultural forces in early modern europe was opera. The stories, songs, and ideas on the stage helped shape national identity for many groups. As part of a project to learn R, I decided to map the premieres of operas across Europe to help create a solid connection between geography, song, and culture.

the Work

There are 4 primary components to the project:

A background map with accurate borders for every given month of the timeline.

A timeline on the left panel that shows a given composer's operas in a chronological list.

Integrated music so that one can listen to the operas as time progresses.

Charts and graphs that show the data in a more compact view.
