the Project

Archives contain some amazing materials—and are often overlooked by search engines. I found a wonderful document compiled in 1948 that detailed the history of streetcars in San Francisco, and how they pushed the development of the city. I wanted to bring the document to life on the web so others could learn about this fantastic bit of history.

the Work

I had originally thought that a scroll-based web product was the optimal solution for this project. However, after some limitations were discovered on the ability of browsers to render the complex layers and animations in Mapbox I realized a different solution was needed.

Ultimately I ended up running the project virtually—capturing each frame and then be compiling them into a video.

It needs a version 2

I had to compromise a great deal on my vision because my technical skills needed improvement. I learned enough to realize the second version would be much closer to the original plan, and I would be better poised for success if I start over from the ground up.

...but it works.

It accomplished the original goal of presenting an archival document to a new audience. I'm pretty pleased with myself. I learned a great deal and when I get the time will make an even better version!

I hope you enjoy it!
